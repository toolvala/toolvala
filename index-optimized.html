1. Introduction

In India, House Rent Allowance (HRA) is one of the most effective tax-saving components for salaried individuals. Under Section 10(13A) of the Income Tax Act, employees living in rented properties can exempt a significant part of their income from taxes. However, the legal prerequisite for this is the submission of a verifiable Rent Receipt. The **Rent Receipt Generator** by **Toolvala.in** is a high-precision digital tool that automates this documentation, ensuring your HRA claims are processed smoothly by HR departments and the Income Tax Department.

2. How to Use This Calculator

Generating a professional receipt (monthly or for a range like January to March) is designed to be simple:

4. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Can I submit one receipt for 3 months? ▼
Yes. Generating a consolidated receipt for a quarter (e.g. Jan to March) is a standard practice and is accepted by the Income Tax Department as long as the total amount is correct.
2. When is a revenue stamp mandatory? ▼
Under the Indian Stamp Act, a ₹1 revenue stamp is mandatory for any cash payment exceeding ₹5,000. For digital payments (UPI/Bank), it is technically optional but often requested by HR for audit trails.
3. Is the Landlord's PAN always required? ▼
PAN is only mandatory if your total annual rent payment exceeds ₹1,00,000. Below this limit, you only need the signed receipt.
4. Can I pay rent to my parents? ▼
Yes. You can pay rent to parents and claim HRA, provided they declare the amount as income in their own ITR. You cannot be a co-owner of the house.
5. What happens if I miss the HR portal deadline? ▼
If you missed the HR submission, you can still claim HRA directly while filing your Income Tax Return (ITR). Keep your Toolvala receipts saved for verification.
6. Is my data secure on Toolvala.in? ▼
Absolutely. We use 100% client-side logic. We never see, store, or transmit your name, PAN, or rent details to our backend.
