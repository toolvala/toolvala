Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Can I merge more than 20 files? ▼
We recommend 20 files at a time to prevent browser memory (RAM) overflow. If you have 40 files, merge them in two batches of 20, then merge those two results together.
2. Will my file quality decrease? ▼
No. This tool acts as a 'Joiner.' It copies the existing data structures of your PDF into a new container, so images and text remain exactly as sharp as they were in the original.
3. How secure is this tool for my bank statements? ▼
It is the most secure method available online. Because the code runs on *your* device, the data is never sent over the internet to us or any third party.
4. Can I merge a PDF with a signed document? ▼
Yes. If you have already used our Sign PDF Online tool, you can bring that signed file here and merge it with other documents without breaking the signature.
5. What happens if the merge fails? ▼
Failures usually happen with 'Password-Protected' or 'Encrypted' PDFs. Ensure your documents are unlocked before attempting to combine them in the browser.
6. Does this work on iPhone and Android? ▼
Yes. Toolvala.in is built on responsive web technology. You can merge PDFs directly from your Files app or Gallery without installing any third-party apps.
