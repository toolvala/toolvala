3. Beating the ATS Bot: Technical Best Practices

Over 75% of resumes are rejected by an automated bot before a human ever see them. To survive the 'Robot Filter,' your document must follow three golden rules:
A. Keyword Alignment: Mirror the specific terminology used in the job description.
B. Logical Hierarchy: Avoid complex tables or overlapping text which break the parsing logic of ATS bots.
C. Universal Headings: Use standard terms like 'Experience' and 'Education' rather than creative alternatives.
