Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Can I merge password-protected PDFs? ▼
For security reasons, you must remove the password from each PDF before uploading them to the browser for merging. Most browsers allow you to "Print to PDF" to create a version without a password.
2. Will the links inside my PDF still work? ▼
Yes! Our merging engine is designed to preserve the document structure, meaning hyperlinks and internal bookmarks are usually maintained in the final merged document.
3. Is there a limit on file size? ▼
The only limit is your browser's memory. We suggest a total combined size of 100MB for the best experience on mobile devices. If your files are too large, try our PDF Compressor.
