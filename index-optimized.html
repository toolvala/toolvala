Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. When is a revenue stamp required? ▼
According to the Indian Stamp Act, a ₹1 Revenue Stamp is mandatory for any cash payment exceeding ₹5,000. For bank transfers or UPI, it is technically not mandatory, but many HR policies still require it for audit readiness.
2. Can I claim HRA if I live with my parents? ▼
Yes! You can pay rent to your parents and claim HRA, provided they own the property and declare the rent as income in their own ITR. You cannot be a co-owner of the property.
3. Is the Landlord's PAN always mandatory? ▼
PAN is only mandatory if your total annual rent exceeds ₹1,00,000 (roughly ₹8,333 per month). For rent below this, only the receipt is sufficient.
