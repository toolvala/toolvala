Convert UPI ID to QR Code and Payment Link

The UPI ID to QR Code & Payment Link Generator is a powerful, free online utility designed to help individuals, freelancers, and small business owners accept digital payments effortlessly. By using this tool, you can instantly convert your standard UPI ID (Virtual Payment Address) into a highly professional, scannable QR code and a clickable payment URL.

Unified Payments Interface (UPI) has revolutionized digital transactions in India. Millions of users rely on apps like Google Pay (GPay), PhonePe, Paytm, and BHIM every day. This tool simplifies the collection process by eliminating manual entry errors and QR scanning confusion.

What is a UPI Payment Link?

A UPI payment link is a specially formatted URL that starts with upi://pay. When a customer clicks this link on their mobile device, it automatically opens their preferred UPI app and pre-fills your payment details (like your UPI ID, Name, and Amount). This allows for a seamless, one-click checkout experience.

Why Use a UPI ID to QR Code Generator?

If you run a shop, sell products online, or collect donations, sending someone just your UPI ID can lead to mistakes. They might type the ID wrong, enter the wrong amount, or send the money to the wrong person. Converting your UPI ID to QR code solves all these problems:

  • Zero Errors: Pre-filling the amount and payee name ensures the customer pays the exact invoice amount to the correct account.
  • Printable Assets: You can download the generated QR code as a high-quality image, print it, and stick it on your shop counter or product packaging.
  • Easy Sharing: The generated payment link can be embedded in PDF invoices, sent via WhatsApp, or linked on your website.
  • Universal Compatibility: The QR codes and links generated here work flawlessly across all NPCI-approved UPI applications.

How to Generate Your UPI Payment Link & QR Code

  1. Enter your valid UPI ID: (e.g., yourname@okicici or 9876543210@ybl). This is the only mandatory field.
  2. Add Payee Name (Optional): Enter your business or personal name to build trust with the payer.
  3. Enter Amount (Optional): If you are collecting a fixed fee (like ₹500 for a service), enter it here. If left blank, the customer can enter the amount themselves.
  4. Add a Note (Optional): Add an invoice number or description for easy tracking.
  5. Generate & Download: Click the generate button. You can now copy the link or download the QR code image directly to your device.
