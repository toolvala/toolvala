2. To Include a Photo or Not?

The inclusion of a **profile photo** on a resume depends largely on your region and industry. In India, Europe, and Asia, a professional headshot is often viewed as a way to build trust and familiarity. In the US and UK, it is often discouraged to prevent unconscious bias. However, for creative roles, sales, and leadership positions, a high-quality photo can make your profile memorable. Our tool allows you to toggle this option easily; if no photo is uploaded, the text automatically expands to utilize the full width of the document.

3. Understanding ATS Optimization

To pass an ATS bot, your resume must follow three golden rules:
A. Keywords: Identify the verbs and nouns used in the job description and weave them into your "Core Competencies" section.
B. Simple Formatting: Avoid complex tables or overlapping images. Bots read text from top to bottom, left to right.
C. Standard Headings: Use universal terms like "Experience" rather than "My Journey."
