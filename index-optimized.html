Income Tax Rules You Must Know

1. The ₹1 Lakh Threshold (Landlord PAN)

As per Circular No. 08/2013 by the CBDT, if your total annual rent exceeds ₹1,00,000, it is mandatory to provide the PAN of the landlord to your employer. If your landlord does not have a PAN, they must provide a signed declaration to that effect. Our generator includes a dedicated field for the PAN to ensure your documentation is audit-ready.

2. Revenue Stamp Requirements

Under the Indian Stamp Act, a ₹1 Revenue Stamp is mandatory if the cash payment per receipt exceeds ₹5,000. Even if you pay via digital modes like UPI or NEFT, many HR departments still request a stamped receipt as a standard operating procedure for physical records. Our tool provides a specific box where you can affix this stamp after printing.

3. Renting from Parents

Can you pay rent to your parents and claim HRA? Yes. The Income Tax Department allows this provided the transaction is genuine. You must generate monthly receipts and ensure your parents declare this rent as 'Income from House Property' in their own ITR. Note: You cannot be a co-owner of the house you are claiming rent for.

Top 5 Tips for a Successful HRA Claim

  1. Digital Footprint: Avoid cash payments. Pay via UPI, IMPS, or Cheque so that you have a secondary bank statement proof to support your Toolvala rent receipt.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Is a computer-generated rent receipt valid? ▼
Yes. Computer-generated receipts are widely accepted by all major companies (TCS, Infosys, Reliance, etc.) and the Income Tax Department, provided they are signed by the landlord.
2. When is a revenue stamp required? ▼
Legally, it is required for cash payments above ₹5,000. However, for digital payments, it is technically not mandatory but often requested by HR for compliance.
3. Can I claim HRA and Home Loan benefits together? ▼
Yes, if you own a house in a different city or can prove you cannot live in your owned house due to work/distance, you can claim both HRA and home loan interest deductions.
4. What if I don't have a Landlord's PAN? ▼
If the rent is below ₹8,333 per month (₹1L/year), PAN is not required. Above this, you must obtain a declaration from the landlord.
5. Does the generator work for commercial rent? ▼
While designed for residential HRA, the format is standard and can be used for small shop or office rent proof as well.
