Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Is my data safe with Toolvala.in? ▼
Yes. Toolvala.in uses 'Local-Only' processing. Your PDF data remains in your device's temporary memory (RAM) and is cleared once you close the browser tab. We cannot see your files.
2. What is the difference between JPG and PNG? ▼
JPG is 'lossy,' meaning it has a smaller file size but can lose some detail. PNG is 'lossless,' providing the highest possible clarity for text and diagrams but resulting in larger file sizes.
3. Can I convert a password-protected PDF? ▼
For security, you must unlock your PDF (remove the password) using a standard PDF editor before uploading it to our browser-side converter.
4. How do I download all pages at once? ▼
Once the conversion is complete, a 'Download All as ZIP' button will appear. Clicking this will bundle all your images into a single compressed folder for easy saving.
5. Can I extract images from a multi-page merit list? ▼
Yes. Our tool is optimized for recruitment sheets. You can specify exactly which pages you want to extract to save space and time.
6. Does this tool work on mobile? ▼
Absolutely. The Toolvala interface is fully responsive. You can convert PDFs directly from your smartphone's gallery or files app.
