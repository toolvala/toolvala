Why compress images? Speed, SEO & bandwidth savings

Large images are the #1 reason for slow websites. Toolvala.in Image Compressor works entirely inside your browser — no server uploads, no privacy risks. You get instant compression for JPG, PNG, and WebP with full control over quality.

How to use

  1. Upload an image (drag & drop supported).
  2. Adjust compression quality (80% is sweet spot).
  3. Choose output format – JPEG for smallest size, WebP for modern sites.
  4. Preview side-by-side and download the optimized image.

All compression is done with HTML5 Canvas and advanced encoding. Your images never leave your device — safe, fast and 100% free.

Pro tip: For PNG screenshots, use JPEG output to reduce size by ~80% without visible loss.
