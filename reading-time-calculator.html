Understanding Reading Speeds

Different content types require different reading speeds:

  • 100 WPM: Technical/scientific material, legal documents
  • 200 WPM: Average reading speed for most adults
  • 250 WPM: Casual reading, fiction, blogs
  • 300-400 WPM: Skimming, familiar content, news articles

Pro Tip for Content Creators

For maximum engagement on social media:

  • Instagram/Facebook: Aim for 15-30 second reading time
  • Twitter/X: 5-15 second reading time (due to character limits)
  • Blog Posts: 3-7 minute reading time for optimal engagement
  • Email Newsletters: 1-3 minute reading time to maintain attention

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why does my text show different word counts on different tools?

Q: Why are URLs excluded from word count?
