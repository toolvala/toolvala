Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is the average reading speed for an adult? ▼
Most adults read at a speed of 200 to 250 words per minute (WPM). For technical or academic content, this drops to 100-150 WPM.
2. Does this tool count emojis and URLs? ▼
No. To ensure high accuracy, our professional algorithm excludes URLs and Emojis from the word count, as they are processed visually rather than read phonetically.
3. Is the calculation different for Hindi or Marathi? ▼
Our tool handles Devanagari scripts perfectly. While reading speeds for different languages can vary slightly, the 200 WPM average remains a reliable standard for general content.
4. Why should I add "Read Time" to my blog? ▼
It sets expectations. A user is more likely to start an article if they know it only takes 3 minutes, rather than wondering if it’s a 20-minute commitment.
5. Can I use this for speech timing? ▼
Yes! For speaking, set the slider to 130-150 WPM. This is the average speed of clear, professional speech.
6. Is my data stored on Toolvala.in? ▼
No. Everything happens in your browser RAM. Your text is never sent to our servers, ensuring 100% privacy for your drafts.
