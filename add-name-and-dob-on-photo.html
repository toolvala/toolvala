What is Add Name and Date on Photo Tool?

This free online tool allows candidates to add their Name and Date of Photo (DOP) or Date of Birth (DOB) on passport-size photos required for UPSC, SSC, IBPS, RRB and other government exams.

Why Date on Photo is Mandatory for SSC & UPSC?

SSC and UPSC require a recent photograph. Printing the date ensures authenticity and prevents impersonation during exam verification.
