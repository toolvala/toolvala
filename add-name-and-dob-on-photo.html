What is Add Name and Date on Photo Tool?

The Add Name and Date on Photo tool is a free online utility designed for Indian government exam aspirants. It allows candidates to print their Name and Date of Photo (DOP) directly on passport-size images required for UPSC, SSC, IBPS, RRB, and other competitive exams.

Why UPSC & SSC Require Date on Photo?

SSC and UPSC mandate that the photograph must be recent, usually not older than 3 months. Printing the Date of Photo ensures authenticity and prevents impersonation.
