Our professional utility is designed for efficiency and privacy. By utilizing the HTML5 Canvas API, the tool renders your input locally on your device. This means your text never touches a server, providing an uncrackable layer of privacy. In auto-size mode, the algorithm dynamically calculates line widths and heights to ensure your final image is perfectly cropped without manual editing.
