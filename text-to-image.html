2. How the Toolvala Algorithm Works

Our professional utility is designed for efficiency and privacy. By utilizing the HTML5 Canvas API, the tool renders your input locally on your device. This means your text never touches a server, providing an uncrackable layer of privacy. The algorithm dynamically calculates line widths and heights to ensure your final image is perfectly cropped without manual editing.

3. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is the Toolvala Text to Image converter free to use? ▼
Yes, this tool is 100% free for both personal and commercial use. There are no watermarks or hidden subscription fees.
Can I generate a transparent background image? ▼
Absolutely. By selecting the 'Transparent' checkbox and exporting as a PNG, you can create high-quality text overlays with no background.
Are my text inputs private? ▼
Yes. Processing happens locally in your browser memory via the Canvas API. Your text is never uploaded to any server.
Which image formats are supported for export? ▼
The tool currently supports high-resolution PNG (recommended for clarity and transparency) and JPG (best for standard backgrounds).
Does it support custom fonts? ▼
The tool is pre-loaded with high-authority fonts like Inter, Playfair Display, and Montserrat, plus any system fonts installed on your device.
What is the maximum resolution I can generate? ▼
You can enter custom dimensions. The maximum resolution is limited only by your browser's hardware capabilities, typically up to 4000px.
