Our tool is designed for two types of user intent: quick fixes and complex data migrations.

The Toolvala engine will iterate through your document and perform all these swaps in a single execution pass, saving you immense time and effort.

3. Understanding Regular Expressions (Regex)

Regex is a sequence of characters that forms a search pattern. It is the ultimate weapon for data cleaning. When you check the "Use Regex" box in our tool, you unlock features like:

  • Digit Removal: Use \d+ in the find box to identify all numbers in your text.
  • Whitespace Normalization: Use \s+ to find multiple spaces and replace them with a single space.
  • Email Extraction: Use complex patterns to find and replace email structures with placeholders for privacy.

4. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

No. Your privacy is our priority. This tool uses 100% client-side JavaScript. All processing happens in your browser's RAM, and the data vanishes the moment you close the tab.
2. What is the limit on text size? ▼
The limit is determined by your device's memory. Most modern browsers can handle files up to 10MB (millions of words) without any noticeable performance lag.
3. Does the tool support Hindi or Marathi text? ▼
Yes. The tool is fully UTF-8 compliant. You can find and replace any international script, including Devanagari, Arabic, and CJK characters, with 100% fidelity.
