What is Simple Interest?

Simple Interest is the most basic method of calculating interest. It is calculated only on the original principal amount and remains the same throughout the investment or loan period.

Simple Interest Formula

SI = (P × R × T) / 100

Monthly & Yearly Interest Explained

This Simple Interest Calculator supports both monthly and yearly interest rates. Monthly rates are automatically converted into yearly rates to ensure accurate calculations.

Example

If you invest ₹10,000 at 1% per month for 6 months:

  • Monthly Rate → Converted to 12% yearly
  • 6 Months → Converted to 0.5 Years

Simple Interest = ₹600

Simple Interest vs Compound Interest

Simple interest is calculated only on principal, while compound interest is calculated on principal plus accumulated interest. For short-term loans, simple interest is commonly used.
