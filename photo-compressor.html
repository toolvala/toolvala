What is a Photo Compressor?

A Photo Compressor is an online tool used to reduce the file size of images without visibly affecting their quality. Large image sizes can cause upload failures, slow website loading, and rejection in online forms. This free photo compressor helps you compress images in KB instantly.

Why Do You Need to Compress Images?

Most government forms, job applications, websites, and mobile apps impose strict image size limits. Uploading an oversized image can lead to errors or rejection. Compressing photos ensures faster uploads, reduced storage usage, and better website performance.
