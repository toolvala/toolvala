Overtime Pay Calculator

What is an Overtime Calculator?

An Overtime Calculator is an online tool that helps employees, workers, freelancers, and HR professionals calculate extra working hours pay. When an employee works beyond standard working hours, they are entitled to additional compensation known as overtime pay.

This overtime calculator allows you to calculate your overtime salary based on hourly wage, total working hours, and overtime rate. It is especially useful for factory workers, IT employees, shift-based staff, and contractual workers.

How Does Overtime Pay Work?

Overtime pay is usually calculated at a higher rate than normal wages. In most organizations, overtime is paid at 1.5x or 2x the regular hourly wage.

  • Standard working hours: 8 hours/day or 40 hours/week
  • Overtime applies after regular working hours
  • Overtime rate depends on company policy or labor laws

Overtime Calculator Formula

Overtime Hours = Total Hours Worked − Regular Hours
Overtime Pay = Overtime Hours × Hourly Wage × Overtime Rate
Total Salary = Regular Pay + Overtime Pay

Overtime Rules in India

In India, overtime rules vary depending on labor laws, industry, and state regulations. Typically, overtime wages are paid at double the ordinary rate for extra hours worked beyond scheduled shifts.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is overtime mandatory?

No, overtime is not mandatory unless mentioned in the employment contract or required during emergencies.

Is overtime taxable?

Yes, overtime income is generally taxable as part of salary income.

Can I calculate monthly overtime?

Yes, simply add total monthly hours and regular hours to get accurate results.
