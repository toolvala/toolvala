What Is a State Wise Tea Calculator?

A state wise tea calculator is an online tool that calculates the exact amount of tea powder, sugar, milk, and water required to make tea for a given number of people based on regional preferences in India.

Why Use an Online Tea Calculator?

Many people prepare tea using guesswork, which often results in inconsistent taste. Sometimes the tea becomes too strong, sometimes too watery, or too sweet. Using a tea calculator ensures balanced taste, saves ingredients, and saves time.

Milk and Water Ratio for Perfect Chai

For most Indian tea styles, the ideal ratio is:

  • Milk: 100 ml per person
  • Water: 150 ml per person

Ideal Boiling Time for Tea

Boiling time plays an important role in taste. The recommended boiling time is 5 to 7 minutes. Over-boiling can make tea bitter, while under-boiling makes it weak.

State Wise Tea Preferences in India

Different states have different tea preferences:

  • Assam & West Bengal: Strong tea with more tea leaves
  • Punjab: Milk-rich kadak chai
  • Gujarat: Sweeter tea
  • South India: Light tea with less sugar
  • Maharashtra & Delhi: Balanced tea
