Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
What are online text tools?
Online text tools are web-based utilities that help you manipulate, format, clean, and analyze text directly in your browser without installing any software. Common examples include word counters, case converters, and duplicate removers.
Are these tools free?
Yes, all tools listed on this page are 100% free to use. There are no hidden charges, subscription fees, or daily usage limits.
Is my data safe?
Absolutely. These tools operate using client-side JavaScript. This means your text is processed entirely within your device's memory (RAM) and is never uploaded to our servers or stored in any database.
