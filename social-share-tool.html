Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I use the Social Share Link Generator?

Simply paste your website URL (e.g., https://example.com) into the input box above and click the button for the social media platform you wish to share on. The tool will automatically open a sharing window.

2. Is this social sharing tool free?

Yes, this tool is 100% free to use for personal and commercial purposes. There are no limits on how many links you can generate or share.

3. Does this tool work on mobile devices?

Absolutely. The tool is mobile-responsive. If you click 'WhatsApp' or 'Telegram' on a phone, it will attempt to open the installed app directly for a seamless experience.

4. Do you store the links I enter?

No. We prioritize your privacy. All processing happens locally in your browser (Client-Side). We do not save, track, or analyze the URLs you paste into the generator.

5. Why isn't the correct image showing when I share to Facebook?

Social platforms look for "Open Graph" (OG) tags on your website. Ensure your website has an og:image tag defined in the head section. Our tool simply passes your URL to the platform; the platform fetches the image from your site.

6. Can I share links to Instagram using this tool?

Currently, Instagram does not support a public web-based "share link" API like Facebook or X. To share on Instagram, you must copy the link and paste it into your Bio or Story manually.
