Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I use the Toolvala Social Share Generator?

Paste your URL (e.g., https://toolvala.in) into the Link box. Optionally, add a custom message. Click the social button of your choice to open the share window.

2. Is this tool free?

Yes, Toolvala.in offers this tool completely free of charge for unlimited use.

3. Why isn't my text showing on LinkedIn?

LinkedIn's API does not support pre-filled text from third-party tools. You will need to type your personal message in the LinkedIn popup window after our tool opens it.

4. Does it work on Android and iPhone?

Yes! The tool is fully responsive. On mobile devices, clicking WhatsApp or Telegram will try to open the app directly rather than the browser website.
