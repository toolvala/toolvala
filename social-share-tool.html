Platform-Specific Guidelines

1. WhatsApp & Telegram: Personal Connection

Messaging apps are the most powerful conversion drivers today. Our WhatsApp link generator utilizes the api.whatsapp.com endpoint to trigger the native app on mobile or the web version on desktop. This is ideal for peer-to-peer sharing where trust is highest.

2. Facebook & LinkedIn: The Visual Feed

Social feeds like Facebook and LinkedIn rely on Open Graph (OG) meta tags. While these platforms do not allow third-party tools to pre-fill the "Status Update" text for security reasons, our tool ensures the URL is passed correctly so the platform can scrape your site's featured image, title, and description perfectly.

3. X (Twitter) & Pinterest: Rapid Discovery

X is built for speed, and Pinterest is built for inspiration. Our X Tweet Generator allows you to include hashtags and mentions within the custom message box. 5. Can I include emojis in my message? ▼
Absolutely! Our tool supports full UTF-8 encoding, meaning your emojis will translate perfectly to WhatsApp, X, and Telegram.
