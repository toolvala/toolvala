How to Add This Signature

For Gmail App (Android/iOS)

The mobile Gmail app has limited HTML support. We recommend adding the signature via Gmail on Desktop first:

  1. On your computer, click "Copy for Gmail" above.
  2. Go to Gmail Settings (Gear Icon) > See all settings.
  3. Scroll to "Signature", Create New, and paste.
  4. Ensure "Signature defaults" are set to your new signature.

It will then automatically appear when you send emails from the app.

For iPhone (Apple Mail)

  1. Tap "Copy for Gmail" above on your phone.
  2. Go to Settings > Mail > Signature.
  3. Long press in the text box and tap "Paste".
  4. Tip: If formatting looks wrong, shake your phone to "Undo" attributes, which often fixes the layout.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Why is my photo not showing in the email?
Some email clients (like Outlook Desktop) block images by default for security. If you used the "Upload" option, the image is embedded in the code. If you used a "Link", ensure the link is publicly accessible. For the most reliable results, host your image on a secure server (https) like Imgur or your company website.
Q: Can I change the size of my photo?
Yes! We have added a Photo Tools section right under the upload button. Use the slider to increase or decrease the size of your profile picture in real-time.
Q: Is this tool free to use?
Yes, this Email Signature Generator is 100% free. There are no watermarks, and we do not store your personal data on our servers.
Q: How do I crop my photo before uploading?
The tool works best with square images. If you need to crop a photo from a rectangle to a square, try our free Passport Photo Resizer before uploading it here.
Q: Does this work on Dark Mode?
We use transparent backgrounds where possible, but email clients handle dark mode differently. We recommend using a dark text color (like black or dark blue) which most email clients will automatically invert to white in Dark Mode to remain readable.
