How to Take a Perfect Photo at Home

Before using the resizer, ensure your source photo is good:

  1. Lighting: Face a window for natural, even light. Avoid shadows on the face or background.
  2. Background: Stand against a plain white wall. If you don't have one, use a white sheet.
  3. Position: Stand 1 meter away from the background. The camera should be at eye level.
  4. Expression: Keep a neutral expression. Mouth closed, eyes open looking directly at the camera.
  5. Attire: Wear dark-colored clothes to contrast with the white background. Avoid uniforms or glasses (unless medically necessary).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Why was my photo rejected?

Common reasons include: shadows on the face, wearing glasses with glare, background not being plain white, or incorrect dimensions. Ensure your source photo is clear.

2. Can I convert a selfie to a passport photo?

Yes, as long as the selfie is taken with good lighting, a plain background, and your shoulders are straight. Do not use filters.

3. Does this tool change the background?

No, this tool resizes and compresses the image. You should ensure the background is already correct (usually white) before uploading.

4. What is the DPI for passport photos?

Most online applications require 300 DPI (Dots Per Inch) for high-quality printing. Our tool maintains high resolution suitable for printing.
