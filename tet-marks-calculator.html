What is TET Marks Calculator?

The TET Marks Calculator is an online tool that helps candidates calculate their marks obtained in the Teacher Eligibility Test (TET). This includes CTET as well as all State TET examinations.

Many candidates feel confused after the exam regarding their expected score and qualification status. This tool solves that problem by providing instant and accurate results.

How Are TET Marks Calculated?

The TET exam consists of 150 multiple-choice questions. Each question carries 1 mark and there is no negative marking.

  • Correct Answer = +1 mark
  • Wrong Answer = 0 mark
  • Unattempted = 0 mark

Total Marks = Number of Correct Answers

CTET & State TET Passing Marks

As per NCTE norms, the qualifying marks are:

  • General Category: 60% (90 marks)
  • OBC / SC / ST: 55% (82 marks)

Most state TET exams follow the same criteria, though some states may offer additional relaxation.

TET Marks vs Qualification

Scoring the cutoff marks means you are TET qualified. However, qualifying TET does not guarantee a teaching job. It only makes you eligible to apply for teaching recruitment.
