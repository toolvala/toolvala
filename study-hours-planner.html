How the Study Hours Planner Works

This study planner calculates your total weekly study hours using daily availability and number of study days. Based on preparation intensity, it divides time equally among subjects and recommends an ideal daily subject-wise study duration.

Why Use a Study Hours Planner?

A study hours planner helps students stay consistent, reduce stress, improve productivity, and track preparation effectively. Planning study time improves retention and exam performance.

Important Note

This planner provides a recommended schedule. Students should take breaks, sleep well, and adjust hours based on personal capacity.
