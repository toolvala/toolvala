Add Signature to Photo Online: Best Practices

To ensure your application is accepted on the first try, follow these best practices before you add signature to photo online:

  • Use a Thick Pen: When signing on paper before scanning, use a thick black or dark blue marker/pen. Faint ballpoint pen signatures often become invisible when compressed.
  • Crop the Signature: Before uploading to the merger tool, crop out any excess white space around your signature. The tighter the crop, the better it will look when merged.
  • Check the KB Size: Most portals require the final merged image to be between 20KB and 50KB. If your merged file is too large, you may need to use an image compressor before uploading it to the exam portal.
