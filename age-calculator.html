About Age Calculator

Age Calculator by Toolvala helps you calculate your exact age from your date of birth. It shows age in years, months, days, hours, minutes, and leap years.

How to Use

  1. Select your date of birth
  2. Click on Calculate Age
  3. View your exact age instantly

Use Cases

  • Government & job forms
  • School & college records
  • Medical & insurance documents
  • Personal curiosity

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Does this calculator count leap years?

Yes, it accurately counts all leap years between your date of birth and today.

Is this tool free?

Yes, it is 100% free and works directly in your browser.

Is my data saved?

No, all calculations are done locally. No data is stored.
