What is a Rent Receipt Generator?

A Rent Receipt Generator is an online tool that allows tenants to create legally acceptable rent receipts instantly. In India, rent receipts are commonly required for claiming House Rent Allowance (HRA) exemption under the Income Tax Act.

Instead of writing rent receipts manually every month, this online rent receipt generator helps you create a professional, printable receipt in seconds. The receipt format generated by Toolvala follows employer-accepted standards.

Why Rent Receipt is Important for HRA

Salaried employees who receive HRA must submit rent receipts as proof of rent payment. Employers use these receipts to calculate and allow HRA exemption under Section 10(13A) of the Income Tax Act.

  • Proof of monthly rent payment
