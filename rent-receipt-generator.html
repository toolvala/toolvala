Rent Receipt Generator – Complete Guide

A Rent Receipt Generator is an online tool that helps tenants create legally valid rent receipts instantly. In India, rent receipts are mainly required for claiming House Rent Allowance (HRA) exemption under the Income Tax Act.

This free rent receipt generator by Toolvala allows you to create A4 printable rent receipt PDFs that are accepted by employers, HR departments, and tax consultants.

Why Rent Receipt Is Important

  • Mandatory for HRA exemption
  • Proof of rent payment
  • Required for payroll processing
  • Helps during income tax scrutiny

Valid Rent Receipt Format in India

A valid rent receipt should include tenant name, landlord name, rental property address, rent amount, rent month, date, and landlord signature. If annual rent exceeds ₹1,00,000, landlord PAN is mandatory.

Is Digital Rent Receipt Accepted?

Yes. Digitally generated rent receipts are accepted by most employers. You can print and sign the receipt manually if required.
