What is a Rent Receipt Generator?

A Rent Receipt Generator is an online tool used to create legally acceptable rent receipts for tenants paying house rent. These receipts are commonly required for claiming HRA exemption under Income Tax in India.

With this free rent receipt generator, you can instantly generate printable rent receipts without any manual formatting. The tool is especially useful for salaried employees, HR departments, and freelancers.

Why is Rent Receipt Important?

  • Required for HRA tax exemption
  • Proof of rent payment
  • Helps during income tax scrutiny
  • Maintains rental transaction records

Rent Receipt for HRA Exemption

If your annual rent exceeds ₹1,00,000, submitting the landlord’s PAN becomes mandatory. This rent receipt generator allows optional PAN entry as per Indian income tax rules.

Who Should Use This Tool?

  • Salaried employees claiming HRA
  • Tenants paying monthly rent
  • HR & payroll teams
  • Accountants & tax consultants

Is Digital Rent Receipt Valid?

Yes. Digitally generated rent receipts are accepted by most employers and are valid for income tax purposes when printed or signed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is landlord signature required?

Some employers require a signature. You can print and sign manually.

Is PAN mandatory?

PAN is mandatory if annual rent exceeds ₹1,00,000.

Can I generate old rent receipts?

Yes, you can select any previous month and year.
