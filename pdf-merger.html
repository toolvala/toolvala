Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Can I merge a PDF and then delete specific pages? ▼
Yes! This is a very common workflow. First, use this tool to combine your files, then take the result to our Delete PDF Pages tool to remove any unwanted content.
2. Is it safe to merge confidential bank statements? ▼
Absolutely. Because the merging happens locally in your browser's RAM, the data is never transmitted to us or any third party. It is the safest way to handle sensitive financial data.
3. Will the combined PDF be too large for email? ▼
If your source files are high-resolution scans, the combined file might be large. In that case, simply use our PDF Compressor to shrink the file to under 25MB for easy emailing.
4. Can I merge PDFs with different orientations? ▼
Yes. Our engine handles mixed Portrait and Landscape orientations perfectly. Each page will retain its original dimensions in the final combined file.

Encrypted files cannot be merged for security reasons. You must remove the password using a PDF editor before uploading the files to the Toolvala browser-side merger.
6. Does the Toolvala PDF Merger work on mobile? ▼
Yes. Our website is fully mobile-responsive. You can merge PDFs directly from your iPhone or Android file manager using your mobile browser.
