Ultimate Guide to WhatsApp Message Formatting

WhatsApp is the most popular messaging app globally, used by billions for personal communication and business dealings. However, many users stick to plain text, unaware that WhatsApp supports rich text formatting. Our WhatsApp Message Formatter tool helps you style your messages effortlessly, making your chats stand out.

Whether you want to highlight an important point, correct a mistake playfully with strikethrough, or send code snippets, mastering WhatsApp formatting is essential. Below is a comprehensive guide on how to use these features manually and why you should use our tool.

Why Format Your WhatsApp Messages?

Formatting isn't just about aesthetics; it is about communication clarity. Here is why you should start formatting today:

  • Emphasis: Use Bold to make headlines or key dates pop out in a long message.
  • Tone: Use Italics for sarcastic comments, book titles, or soft emphasis.
  • Corrections: Use Strikethrough to show changes in plans or prices without deleting the original context.
  • Technical Data: Use Monospace for sharing bank account numbers, UPI IDs, or coding snippets, as it makes characters distinct and easier to copy.

How to Format WhatsApp Text Manually

If you don't have our tool handy, you can type special characters to format text. Here is the cheat sheet:

1. Bold Text

To make text bold, place an asterisk * on both sides of the text.

  • Type: *Hello World*
  • Result: Hello World

2. Italic Text

To italicize your message, place an underscore _ on both sides of the text.

  • Type: _Hello World_
  • Result: Hello World

3. Strikethrough Text

To strike through text, place a tilde ~ on both sides of the text.

  • Type: ~Hello World~
  • Result: Hello World

4. Monospace Text

To apply the typewriter font (Monospace), place three backticks ``` on both sides of the text.

  • Type: ```Hello World```
  • Result: Hello World

5. Combining Formats

You can combine formats by nesting the symbols. Always remember to close the symbols in the reverse order you opened them.

  • Type: *_Bold and Italic_*
  • Result: Bold and Italic

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can I use these formats on WhatsApp Web?

Yes, WhatsApp formatting works seamlessly on Android, iOS, WhatsApp Web, and the Desktop App. The syntax remains exactly the same across all platforms.

Does this tool store my messages?

No. This tool runs entirely in your browser (client-side). We do not save, record, or transmit your messages to any server. Your privacy is 100% secure.

Why isn't my formatting working in WhatsApp?

Common reasons include:

  • Putting a space between the symbol and the text (e.g., * Bold * will not work; *Bold* will).
  • Forgetting to close the tag.
  • Using unsupported special characters inside the tags.

Is there a limit to how much text I can format?

WhatsApp has a character limit of around 65,536 characters per message. Our tool can handle large blocks of text within reason, but keep your messages concise for better readability.

Does this work for WhatsApp Status?

Yes, you can use Bold, Italic, Strikethrough, and Monospace formatting in text-based WhatsApp Status updates as well.
