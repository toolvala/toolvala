What is EMI?

EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment. It is the fixed amount paid by a borrower to the lender every month until the loan is fully repaid. EMI includes both principal and interest components.

EMI Calculation Formula

EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N − 1]

Where P is loan amount, R is monthly interest rate, and N is number of monthly installments.
