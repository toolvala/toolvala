How to Write a CV that Gets You Hired in 2025

Recruiters today value clarity over creativity. Here are the top tips for using our tool effectively:

  • Keep it Concise: Try to fit your CV onto one or two pages. Our template creates a compact sidebar for contact info and skills, leaving the main area free for your work history.
  • Focus on Achievements: In the "Description" fields, use bullet points. Don't just list duties; list results. (e.g., "Increased sales by 20%" rather than "Responsible for sales").
  • Optimize for Keywords: Use the "Skills" section to list specific technologies or competencies relevant to the job description. These are the keywords ATS bots look for.
