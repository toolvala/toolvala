What is a Loan Interest Calculator?

A loan interest calculator is an online financial tool that helps borrowers estimate the cost of a loan before applying. It calculates how much interest you will pay over the loan tenure and how much your monthly EMI will be. Toolvala.in provides a fast, accurate, and user-friendly loan calculator suitable for Indian financial needs.

How Does Loan Interest Work?

Loan interest is the cost charged by banks or lenders for borrowing money. It is usually calculated on a monthly reducing balance. Your EMI consists of two parts – principal repayment and interest. In the early years, interest is higher, while later EMIs include more principal repayment.
