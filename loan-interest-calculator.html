What Is Simple Interest?

Simple Interest is the most basic method of calculating interest on a loan or investment. It is calculated only on the original principal amount and remains constant throughout the entire period.

Why Use Toolvala.in Simple Interest Calculator?

The Toolvala.in Simple Interest Calculator is designed specifically for Indian users. It supports both monthly and yearly interest rates, making it useful for bank loans as well as private lending.

Simple Interest Formula

Simple Interest = (Principal × Rate × Time) / 100

Where Simple Interest Is Commonly Used

  • Personal loans
  • Short-term borrowing
  • Village and private lending
  • Basic financial education
