How This Tea Calculator Works

The calculator uses a simple formula:

Total Cups = (Current Age − Start Age) × 365 × Cups per Day

Total volume is calculated using average cup size in milliliters.

Example Calculation

  • Started drinking tea at age: 15
  • Current age: 40
  • Cups per day: 3
  • Total years: 25
