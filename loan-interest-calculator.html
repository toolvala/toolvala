What is a Loan Interest Calculator?

A Loan Interest Calculator is a financial tool that helps you calculate the total interest payable on a loan over its entire tenure. It is useful for personal loans, home loans, education loans, and car loans.

Why Use a Loan Interest Calculator?

  • Understand total interest cost
  • Compare loan offers easily
  • Plan finances better
  • Avoid hidden interest surprises
