About TET Eligibility Checker Tool

The Teacher Eligibility Test (TET) is a mandatory examination for candidates aspiring to become teachers in government and aided schools across India. This TET Eligibility Checker is designed to help candidates instantly verify their eligibility before applying for CTET or any State TET.

Many aspirants waste time and money applying without knowing eligibility rules. This tool solves that problem by checking age and qualification as per official guidelines.

What is CTET and State TET?

CTET (Central Teacher Eligibility Test) is conducted by CBSE, while State TET exams are conducted by respective state governments such as MAHA TET, UP TET, REET, TSTET, and more.

Both exams follow norms prescribed by the National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE).

TET Eligibility Criteria (As per NCTE)

Primary Teacher (Class 1–5)

  • Minimum age: 18 years
  • Qualification: D.El.Ed / D.Ed / BTC

Upper Primary Teacher (Class 6–8)

  • Minimum age: 18 years
  • Qualification: B.Ed or equivalent
