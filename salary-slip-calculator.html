What is a Salary Slip?

A salary slip, also known as a payslip, is an official document issued by an employer to an employee every month. It contains detailed information about salary components such as basic pay, allowances, deductions, and net salary. A salary slip is an essential financial document used for income proof, loan applications, tax filing, and employment verification.

Salary Components Explained

Basic Salary: The fixed core component of your salary.

HRA (House Rent Allowance): Provided to employees to cover rental expenses.

DA (Dearness Allowance): Cost-of-living adjustment allowance.

Other Allowances: Includes travel allowance, medical allowance, special allowance, etc.

Provident Fund (PF): Retirement benefit deduction.

Professional Tax: State-level tax deducted monthly.

Income Tax: Tax deducted as per income slab.
