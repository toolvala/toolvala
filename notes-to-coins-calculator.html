Top 5 Tips for Error-Free Cash Tallying

  1. Double-Count Large Bills: Always count your ₹500 and ₹200 stacks twice before entering the quantity into the calculator.
  2. Use the 'Rubber Band' Method: For large volumes, group notes into bundles of 10 or 20 and secure them. This makes the input process much faster.
  3. Separate Soil and Damaged Notes: Keep torn or heavily soiled notes in a separate pile as they often need special processing at the bank.
  4. Audit mid-day: Don't wait for closing. A quick 5-minute tally at noon using Toolvala can catch errors early.
  5. Digital Backup: Once you've tallied your cash, take a screenshot of our result area to keep as a digital record for your accountant.
