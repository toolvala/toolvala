Frequently Asked Questions

Is this calculator accurate for all currencies?

Yes! Our calculator uses official denomination data for each supported currency and allows custom denomination setups for any currency.

Can I add custom denominations?

Absolutely! Use the "Custom Denominations" section to select exactly which coins and notes to include in your calculations.

How does the calculator determine the breakdown?

We use a greedy algorithm that starts with the largest denomination and works down, similar to how cash registers operate in real life.

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, completely free with no registration, limits, or hidden costs. All calculations happen in your browser.
