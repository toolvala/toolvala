How Toolvala.in Calculates Leap Years

Simple math often fails when calculating long durations because of leap years. A year is not exactly 365 days; it’s approximately 365.2425 days. Our Anniversary Calculator uses advanced date-logic libraries to ensure that February 29th is accounted for, giving you 100% accurate results even for 50-year calculations.
