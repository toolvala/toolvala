What is a Home Loan Calculator?

A Home Loan Calculator is a financial tool that helps you calculate the monthly EMI (Equated Monthly Installment), total interest payable, and total loan amount for a housing loan. It allows borrowers to plan finances effectively before applying for a home loan.

Why Use a Home Loan EMI Calculator?

  • Know your monthly EMI in advance
  • Compare different loan offers
  • Plan monthly budget easily
  • Understand total interest payable
