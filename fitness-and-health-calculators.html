Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) estimates whether your weight is healthy for your height.

2. What is Body Fat Percentage?

Body fat percentage measures how much of your total body weight consists of fat.

3. What is BMR?

Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is the number of calories your body burns while completely at rest.

4. Are these calculators free?

Yes. Every calculator on ToolVala is completely free.

5. Can I use these calculators on mobile?

Yes. They are fully responsive and work on phones, tablets, and desktops.

6. Is my data stored?

No. Calculations are performed in your browser.

7. Which BMI range is healthy?

A BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 is generally considered healthy for adults.

8. Why should I calculate BMR?

BMR helps estimate your daily calorie requirements.

9. Which calculator should I use first?

Most users begin with the BMI Calculator and then use the BMR and Body Fat calculators.

10. Are these results medical advice?

No. These calculators are educational tools and should not replace professional medical advice.
