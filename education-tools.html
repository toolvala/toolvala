Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
How is Attendance Percentage calculated?
It is calculated by dividing the number of classes attended by the total classes held, multiplied by 100. Our tool also calculates "Classes to Bunk" to maintain a specific target (e.g., 75%).
Is the TET Eligibility Checker updated?
Yes, our tool incorporates the latest NCTE guidelines for CTET and State TET eligibility criteria effective for the year 2026.
Are these tools free?
Yes, all education tools listed here are free for students and aspirants.
