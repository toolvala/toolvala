Understanding Time Formats: Decimal Hours vs. Minutes

One of the most confusing aspects of payroll is converting minutes to decimals. Most accounting software uses decimal hours.

The Conversion Logic

There are 60 minutes in an hour. To convert minutes to a decimal, you divide the minutes by 60.

  • 15 minutes = 15 / 60 = 0.25 hours
  • 30 minutes = 30 / 60 = 0.50 hours
  • 45 minutes = 45 / 60 = 0.75 hours
  • 10 minutes = 10 / 60 = 0.17 hours

This calculator automatically handles this conversion for you, ensuring that when you see "40.5 hours," you know it means 40 hours and 30 minutes, not 40 hours and 50 minutes.
