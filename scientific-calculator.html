Troubleshooting & FAQ

How do I type exponents? Use the ^ symbol. For example, 2 to the power of 3 is 2^3.

Why is my Sin() result weird? Check the DEG/RAD toggle. In Scientific mode, ensure you are in the correct angle mode for your problem.
