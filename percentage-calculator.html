Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is the standard percentage formula? ▼
The formula is: (Value / Total Value) × 100. This tells you what portion of the whole is represented by your value.
2. How to calculate percentage increase? ▼
Percentage increase = [(New Value - Old Value) / Old Value] × 100. Our tool does this automatically in the 'Percentage Change' tab.
3. Can I use this for GST calculation? ▼
Yes. To find 18% GST, use the 'Basic Percentage' mode with 18% of your product price.
4. Is this calculator free? ▼
Yes, Toolvala provides all financial and math tools 100% free with no registration required.
5. What is 'Reverse Percentage'? ▼
Reverse percentage is finding the original value after an increase or decrease. While this tool focuses on forward calculation, you can find the original by using the results to cross-check.
6. Does it work for exam marks? ▼
Absolutely. The 'Exam Marks' tab is specifically designed to convert scores into percentages for school and college results.
