Understanding Percentage Calculations

In our daily lives, percentages are everywhere. From calculating the tip at a restaurant to understanding your annual salary hike, percentages help us compare numbers effectively. The **Toolvala Percentage Calculator** simplifies these calculations into three easy-to-use modes.

1. Percentage Increase / Decrease

This is the most common calculation in finance and business. Whether you are tracking stock prices, inflation, or your savings growth, you need to know the *rate of change*.

The Formula: ((New Value - Old Value) / Old Value) × 100

If the result is positive, it's an Increase. If negative, it's a Decrease. Our calculator handles the math instantly.

2. Calculating Exam Marks

Students often need to convert their scored marks into a percentage to know their grade. Simply dividing your score by the total marks and multiplying by 100 gives you the exact percentage.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I calculate percentage increase? ▼
Subtract the original number from the new number. Divide that difference by the original number. Multiply by 100.
2. How to calculate marks percentage? ▼
Divide your obtained marks by the total maximum marks, then multiply the result by 100.
3. Can I use this for GST? ▼
