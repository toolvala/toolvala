What is Percentage?

A percentage is a number expressed as a fraction of 100. The word percentage comes from the Latin term per centum, meaning “by the hundred”. Percentages are widely used in academics, finance, business, statistics, and everyday life.

Percentage Formula

Percentage = (Obtained Value / Total Value) × 100
