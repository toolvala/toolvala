About Marks Needed to Pass Calculator

The Marks Needed to Pass Calculator is a simple yet powerful academic tool designed for students who want to know the minimum marks required to pass an exam. Why Knowing Passing Marks Is Important

Understanding the minimum passing marks helps students plan their studies efficiently. Many students fail not because of lack of ability, but due to improper planning and confusion about pass criteria. This tool removes guesswork and gives exact numbers.

Passing Percentage Rules in India

In India, most boards and universities follow these passing criteria:

  • School exams: 33% to 35%
  • Board exams (CBSE/State): 33%
  • University exams: 35% to 40%
  • Competitive exams: varies by category
