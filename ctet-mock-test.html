What is CTET Mock Test?

The CTET Mock Test is a practice examination designed to simulate the actual Central Teacher Eligibility Test. It helps candidates understand the exam pattern, question difficulty and time management.

Why CTET Mock Test is Important?

  • Improves exam confidence
  • Identifies weak areas
  • Enhances speed and accuracy
  • Reduces exam fear

CTET Exam Pattern Overview

CTET consists of two papers:

  • Paper I: For Primary Teachers (Class 1–5)
  • Paper II: For Upper Primary Teachers (Class 6–8)

Each paper contains 150 MCQs carrying 1 mark each. There is no negative marking.
