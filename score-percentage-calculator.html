How to Calculate Percentage of Marks: The Complete Guide

Whether you are a student waiting for board exam results, a college graduate calculating your aggregate for a job application, or a teacher grading papers, calculating percentages is a fundamental skill. The Toolvala.in Score Percentage Calculator simplifies this process, ensuring accuracy and saving time.

The Basic Percentage Formula

The mathematical formula to calculate the percentage of marks is straightforward:

Percentage (%) = (Marks Obtained / Total Marks) × 100

For example, if you scored 450 marks out of a total of 500:

  • Divide 450 by 500 = 0.9
  • Multiply 0.9 by 100 = 90%

CGPA to Percentage

Often, CBSE and college students receive results in CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average). To convert CGPA to percentage, the standard multiplier is usually 9.5.

Formula: Percentage = CGPA × 9.5

For example, if your CGPA is 8.0, your percentage is 8.0 × 9.5 = 76%.
