Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is round-off in mathematics?

Round-off is the process of approximating a number to a specified number of digits or decimal places.

2. Can I round numbers to specific decimal places?

Yes, just enter the required number of decimal places and choose a rounding method.

3. What is the difference between round, floor, and ceil?

Round gives the nearest value, floor rounds down, and ceil rounds up.
