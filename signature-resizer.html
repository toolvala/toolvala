What is a Signature Resizer?

A Signature Resizer is an online tool used to resize and compress scanned or photographed signatures to meet exact size requirements demanded by government forms, exams, banks, and job portals.

Why Signature Size Matters?

Most official forms reject signatures that exceed size limits such as 20KB, 50KB, or 100KB. Incorrect dimensions or background colors can also cause upload failure.

Common Signature Requirements

  • File size: 20KB – 100KB
  • Background: White
  • Format: JPG / JPEG / PNG
  • Clear black or blue ink

Signature Resizer with KB Target

Is This Tool Secure?

Yes. All processing happens inside your browser. No signature image is uploaded to any server.
