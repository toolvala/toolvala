What is TET Study Planner?

The TET Study Planner is an online tool designed to help candidates systematically prepare for the Teacher Eligibility Test (TET). It creates a balanced daily study schedule covering all subjects as per the CTET and State TET syllabus.

Many aspirants fail not due to lack of knowledge but due to lack of proper planning. A structured study plan improves consistency and confidence.

Why Study Planning is Important for TET?

  • Helps manage time efficiently
  • Covers full syllabus without stress
  • Improves revision quality
  • Prevents last-minute panic

CTET & State TET Syllabus Overview

Both CTET and State TET exams include the following subjects:

  • Child Development and Pedagogy
  • Language I
  • Language II
  • Mathematics
  • Environmental Studies / Science / Social Science

How This TET Study Planner Works

This planner calculates the number of days remaining and divides your daily study time equally across all subjects. You can adjust hours as per your comfort.
