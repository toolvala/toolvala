4. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How is the hourly to annual salary calculated? ▼
Our tool follows the standard business logic: (Hourly Rate × Hours per Week) × 52 Weeks. This is the unadjusted gross income formula used by major payroll systems globally.
2. Does the calculator account for taxes and PF? ▼
No. This tool calculates Gross Salary (the amount before any deductions). Your Net (Take-Home) salary will be lower due to income tax, professional tax, and provident fund contributions.
3. What is a Bi-Weekly pay period? ▼
Bi-weekly means you are paid once every 14 days. This results in 26 paychecks per year. It is different from 'Semi-Monthly' (24 paychecks), which is pay on the 1st and 15th.
4. How do I calculate my daily rate? ▼
If you are salaried, our tool takes your annual total and divides it by (52 weeks × your working days). It is a vital metric for understanding the value of your workday.
5. Is my data secure on Toolvala.in? ▼
Yes. Toolvala uses client-side JavaScript processing. Your income data never leaves your device and is never uploaded to any external server.
6. Can I use this for freelance projects? ▼
Absolutely. Freelancers often work varying hours. Use the tool to find your 'Effective Hourly Rate' by entering your project fee as a monthly or weekly amount and adjusting your actual hours worked.
