How Word Counter Tool Work?

The Word Counter operates directly inside your web browser using lightweight, real-time JavaScript logic. Here is what happens under the hood when you paste your text:

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Does this word counter support Hindi text?
Yes! Unlike many basic tools, our counter uses Unicode-safe logic to accurately count words written in Devanagari script, correctly identifying matras, combined characters, and the Purna Viram (।) for sentences.
2. Is my text saved or uploaded?
No. The tool is 100% browser-based. Your text is processed locally on your device and is never uploaded or saved to any external servers, ensuring complete privacy.
3. How is the reading time calculated?
Reading time is estimated based on the average adult reading speed of 200 words per minute. The tool calculates the exact seconds and minutes required to read your text and displays it dynamically.
4. What is the difference between word count and character count?
Word count is the total number of words in your text. Character count is the total number of individual letters, numbers, symbols, and spaces. For example, the phrase "Hello World" is 2 words but 11 characters.
5. Does the tool count spaces as characters?
Yes, standard character counting includes spaces. However, our dashboard also provides a separate 'Characters (No Spaces)' metric if you need a strict count of just the visible letters and symbols.
6. Why are character limits important for social media?
Platforms like X (formerly Twitter), Instagram, YouTube, and LinkedIn have strict character limits for posts, bios, and titles. Using a word counter ensures your text fits perfectly without getting abruptly cut off or generating an error upon posting.
