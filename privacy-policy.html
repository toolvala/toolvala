Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. This policy explains how Toolvala.in handles your information.

Last Updated: December 1, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Toolvala.in ("we," "our," or "us"). We operate the website toolvala.in (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

Important Notice: Toolvala.in is designed with privacy as a core principle. Our tools run entirely in your browser (client-side), meaning your data never leaves your device. We don't have servers that process or store your information.

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Data

We do not collect any personal identification information. You can use all tools without registering, logging in, or providing any personal details such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Mailing address
  • Payment information (all tools are 100% free)

2.2 Usage Data

We do not track your usage. Unlike many websites, we do not use:

  • Cookies for tracking
  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • User behavior tracking
  • Session recording
  • IP address tracking

2.3 Data You Provide to Tools

When you use our tools (like text converters, calculators, etc.), the data you input:

  • Stays entirely in your browser
  • Is never sent to our servers
  • Is not stored by us in any way
  • Disappears when you close the browser tab (unless you save it locally)

2.4 Information Collected by Our Hosting Provider

Toolvala.in is hosted on GitHub Pages. As our hosting provider, GitHub may automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our site, such as your IP address, browser type, referring pages, and visit timestamps. This data is used for operational and security purposes. For details, please refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement.

3. How We Use Information

Since we don't collect personal data, there's no data for us to use. Our service architecture ensures:

  • No data processing: All computations happen in your browser
  • No data storage: We don't have databases storing user information
  • No data sharing: We have nothing to share with third parties
  • No profiling: We don't create user profiles or track preferences

5. Cookies Policy

Toolvala.in does not use cookies for tracking purposes. We don't use:

  • Analytics cookies
  • Advertising cookies
  • Session cookies

The only cookies that might be present are:

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect font and icon display.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13 ("Children"). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. Since we don't collect any personal data, this is inherently protected.

If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your Child has provided us with Personal Data, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected Personal Data from children without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our servers (though we don't have servers storing such data).

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Our unique advantage: Since all tool processing happens in your browser, your data never travels over the internet to our servers. This provides inherent security benefits:

  • No data transmission risks
  • No server breaches can expose your data
  • No database hacking risks
  • Your data stays on your device
