How to Use the Signature Generator

Method 1: Draw Your Signature

This method is best if you want to replicate your actual wet-ink signature. It works perfectly on mobile devices, tablets (like iPads), and laptops with touchscreens. You can also use a mouse on a desktop computer.

  1. Select the "Draw Signature" tab.
  2. Choose your ink color (Black, Blue, Red, or Green).
  3. Adjust the pen thickness using the slider.
  4. Draw on the canvas. If you make a mistake, hit "Clear" and try again.
  5. Click "Download PNG" to save it to your device.

Method 2: Type Your Signature

If you don't have a steady hand or prefer a cleaner look, use the typing feature. We have curated a selection of beautiful cursive and handwriting fonts.

  1. Select the "Type Signature" tab.
  2. Type your name in the input box.
  3. Click through the different "Style" buttons to see how your signature looks in different fonts (e.g., Dancing Script, Pacifico).
  4. Adjust the color and size, then download.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is this legally binding?

In most jurisdictions (like the US and EU), an electronic signature (e-signature) created via a generator like this is legally binding for general business contracts, agreements, and invoices. However, for highly sensitive documents (like wills or government IDs), a cryptographic Digital Signature might be required.

Can I change the color to Blue?

Yes! Blue ink is often preferred for official documents to distinguish the original from a photocopy. Simply click the Blue dot in the controls section before downloading.

What format is the download?

The file is downloaded as a PNG (Portable Network Graphics). This format supports transparency, ensuring your signature blends perfectly onto any document.
