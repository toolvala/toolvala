2. To Include a Photo or Not?

Adding a **profile photo to your resume** is a nuanced choice based on your location and industry:

  • India, Europe, and UAE: A professional photo is standard and helps build a personal connection and trust.
  • USA and UK: Traditionally discouraged to prevent unconscious bias, though becoming popular in creative and sales industries.

Our tool allows you to toggle this option. If you upload a photo, the layout shifts to a balanced executive style. If you don't, the contact info expands to create a powerful text-centric document.

3. Beating the ATS Gatekeeper

Over 75% of resumes are rejected by software before a human sees them. To win in 2026, your document must follow these rules:
A. Keywords: Mirror the terminology in the job description.
B. Simple Logic: Avoid complex tables or overlapping text which break the parsing logic of ATS bots.
C. Standard Headers: Use universal terms like "Experience" and "Education."
