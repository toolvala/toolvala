Online Biodata Maker: Create Professional CVs & Resumes for Free

In today's competitive job market—or when searching for a life partner in many cultures—presentation is everything. A Biodata (Biographical Data) acts as your first impression. Unlike a complex CV, a biodata focuses on personal details, physical attributes, and background summary alongside professional qualifications.

Our Biodata Maker Tool is a fast, secure, and free solution designed to help you create stunning profiles in minutes without needing complex software like Word or Photoshop.

Why Use an Online Biodata Creator?

  • Instant Formatting: No need to struggle with alignment or margins. Our tool places your photo, contact info, and details in a professional layout automatically.
  • Live Preview: See changes instantly as you type. The split-screen interface ensures you know exactly how your document will look before you print.
  • Privacy First: This tool runs Client-Side. Your personal data and photo are processed in your browser and are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Free PDF Download: With a single click, you can save your biodata as a PDF file to share via email or WhatsApp.

Differences Between Resume, CV, and Biodata

While often used interchangeably, there are nuances:

  1. Resume: A 1-page summary of work experience and skills (common in the USA).
  2. CV (Curriculum Vitae): A detailed academic and professional history (common in Europe/Academic fields).
  3. Biodata: Focuses on personal specifics (Date of Birth, Religion, Marital Status, Height/Weight). It is widely used in South Asia for marriage proposals and government job applications.

How to Create the Perfect Biodata

To get the best results from this tool, upload a high-quality, professional photo (headshot). Keep your "About Me" summary concise but descriptive. Ensure your contact details are accurate. When you are finished, simply click the "Download PDF" button. In the print dialog settings, ensure "Background Graphics" is checked to keep the colors and styling intact.
