Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Is there a scan limit on these QR codes? ▼
No. These are static QR codes. Since they encode the data directly, they can be scanned an unlimited number of times for life. We do not charge per scan.
2. Can I change the URL after I print the QR code? ▼
No. Static QR codes encode the specific URL into the pattern. To change the link later, you would need a 'Dynamic QR' service. However, you can use a URL shortener like Bitly as your target to maintain future flexibility.
3. Which colors are best for scanning? ▼
High contrast is key. A dark foreground (QR dots) on a light background is the most reliable. Avoid using light yellow or pastels for the QR pattern as some cameras may struggle to detect them.
4. Do these QR codes expire? ▼
Never. The code is a permanent mathematical representation of your data. It will remain valid as long as the printed medium is legible.
5. Can I use these for commercial products? ▼
Yes. All QR codes generated on Toolvala.in are free for commercial, professional, and personal use with no license fees required.
6. Is this tool safe for sharing WiFi passwords? ▼
Extremely safe. Because the processing happens on your own device (client-side), your password is never transmitted to us. This makes it significantly safer than traditional online generators.
