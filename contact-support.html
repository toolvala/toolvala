How to Get Help

Found a bug or having trouble with a specific tool? Please include:

  • Tool name

  • Describe the tool or feature

  • Privacy concerns

  • Other questions

What to Include in Your Message

For faster resolution, please include these details in your support request:

  • Clear subject line (e.g., "Bug Report: Word Counter Tool")
  • Detailed description of the issue or request
  • Steps to reproduce the issue (if applicable)
  • Browser information (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.)
  • Device information (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Screenshots (if helpful to explain the issue)

Typical Response Time: We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. Complex issues may require additional time for investigation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to create an account to contact support?

No, Toolvala doesn't require accounts for anything. You can contact support directly via email without any registration or login.

Is there phone support available?

Currently, we only offer email support at toolvala@gmail.com. This allows us to keep Toolvala completely free while still providing quality support.

Can I get a refund for Toolvala?

Toolvala is 100% free and always will be. There are no payments, subscriptions, or fees, so refunds are not applicable.

How do I report a security vulnerability?

If you discover a security vulnerability, please email us immediately at toolvala@gmail.com with "SECURITY" in the subject line. Please do not disclose security issues publicly until we've had a chance to address them.

Do you offer technical support for tool integration?

We provide basic support for using Toolvala's tools on our website. For advanced integration or API support (if available in the future), please contact us with specific details about your project.

Before Contacting Support

Quick Self-Help Tips

Many common issues can be resolved quickly without waiting for support:

  • Refresh the page: Some temporary issues are resolved with a simple refresh
  • Clear browser cache: Old cached files can sometimes cause issues
  • Try a different browser: Check if the issue is browser-specific
  • Check your internet connection: Ensure you have a stable connection
  • Disable browser extensions: Some extensions can interfere with website functionality

What We Cannot Help With

To manage expectations, here's what our support team cannot assist with:

  • Issues unrelated to Toolvala's website or tools
  • Personal computer or network troubleshooting
  • Third-party software or service issues
  • Legal advice beyond our policies
  • Unlimited custom tool development requests
