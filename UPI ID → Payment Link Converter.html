Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about UPI payment links

What is a UPI payment link?

A UPI payment link is a shareable URL that, when clicked, opens a UPI app with pre-filled payment details. It simplifies the payment process by eliminating the need for the payer to manually enter UPI ID, amount, or other details.

Is it safe to share UPI payment links?

Yes, UPI payment links are secure. They only contain payment information and don't expose sensitive bank details. The payer still needs to authenticate the transaction using their UPI PIN in their app. Always verify the payment details before confirming any transaction.

Do I need a merchant account to use UPI payment links?

No, you don't need a merchant account. Any individual with a UPI ID can generate and share payment links. This tool works with personal UPI accounts from banks, Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and other UPI apps.

Can I set an expiration time for payment links?

Standard UPI payment links don't expire, but some UPI apps may offer time-bound payment requests. For recurring or time-sensitive payments, it's best to generate fresh links. Our tool allows you to create new links anytime.

What if the payer doesn't have the same UPI app as me?

UPI payment links work across all UPI apps. When someone clicks your payment link, it will open in whatever UPI app they have installed. If they don't have any UPI app, they'll be prompted to install one.

Are there any charges for using UPI payment links?

No, generating and using UPI payment links is completely free for both senders and receivers. This tool is also free with no hidden charges. UPI transactions themselves are free for personal use, though merchants may incur small charges.

Can I track payments made through my links?

You'll receive notifications in your UPI app for all payments, just like any other UPI transaction. For better tracking, include unique identifiers (like invoice numbers) in the transaction note when generating links.

What's the maximum amount for UPI payment links?

UPI transaction limits are set by individual banks and typically range from ₹1 lakh to ₹2 lakh per transaction. Some banks may have lower limits. Check with your bank for specific UPI transaction limits.
