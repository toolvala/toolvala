UPI Payment Link & QR Code Generator — Why use it?

Unified Payments Interface (UPI) is the fastest, most convenient digital payment method across India. A UPI payment link simplifies transactions: instead of asking customers to type details, you provide a link or QR code that pre-fills the payee and amount in their UPI app. This reduces friction, speeds checkout, and improves conversion for small businesses, freelancers, and creators.

How it works (simple)

  1. Provide your UPI ID and optionally an amount, payee name and note.
  2. Click Generate Link & QR. The tool builds a UPI deep link: upi://pay?pa=yourupi@bank&am=100&tn=order+123&cu=INR.
  3. The link opens any UPI app (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM). The user approves the payment from their bank app.
  4. You can also download the QR code or share the link via WhatsApp/email/website.

Use cases

  • Local shops & cafes: Place the QR on the counter to accept quick payments.
  • Freelancers: Send a pay link with invoices or contracts.
  • Social sellers: Share payment links on Instagram/WhatsApp status for buyers to pay quickly.
  • Donations: NGOs and community events can collect money securely.
  • Events & registrations: Collect fees and deposits with minimal friction.

Security & best practices

  • Never share your full bank account number in public; UPI ID is safe to share for receiving payments.
  • Verify incoming payments in your bank/UPI app before delivering goods or services.
  • Use a unique payment note or invoice number to reconcile payments easily.

How to integrate on your website

Copy the generated UPI link and use it as an <a href="upi://pay?..."> link or add the QR image to your checkout page. On mobile devices the link will open the supported UPI app directly.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Is this QR secure?
The QR encodes a UPI deep link that only contains payer instructions (UPI ID, amount, note) — actual transfer requires user confirmation in their UPI app and bank authentication (PIN/biometrics).
2. Which apps accept this UPI link?
Major UPI apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, Amazon Pay and other UPI-enabled bank apps support deep linking to process payments.
3. Can I change the amount after generating?
You can regenerate with a different amount. If the link omits amount, payer can enter their desired amount at payment time.
4. Do I need to register or sign in?
No — the generator runs entirely in your browser and does not require login. Nothing is stored on a server.
5. Can someone misuse my UPI ID from the link?
No. A UPI ID alone cannot move money. The payer must approve each payment from their bank account.
6. How do I download the QR as an image?
Use the "Download QR (PNG)" button — it converts the generated QR to a PNG and downloads it to your device.
7. Will it work offline?
The generator runs offline in-browser once the page is loaded. Sharing requires network access.
8. Can I embed the link in an invoice?
Yes — add the generated UPI link as a clickable URL or include the downloaded QR image in your invoice PDF.
9. Does the tool support currency other than INR?
UPI is India-specific (INR). The tool sets the currency to INR (cu=INR).
10. Any fees charged?
No — generating links and QR codes with this tool is free. However, your bank/UPI app may have its own policies for transaction limits or fees.
