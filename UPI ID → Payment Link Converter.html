What is a UPI Payment Link & QR?

Unified Payments Interface (UPI) is the most widely used instant payment system across India. A UPI payment link is a URL structured to instruct UPI apps to prefill the payee (UPI ID), amount and optional note. A QR code encodes this link so a payer can simply scan and complete the payment from their UPI app (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, etc.).

Why use a payment link or QR?

Payment links and QR codes remove friction. Instead of asking a customer to type UPI details manually, provide a link or QR that opens their UPI app with fields filled. This reduces errors, speeds checkout and improves business conversion for offline & online sellers: street vendors, salons, cafes, freelance professionals, social sellers and NGOs.
