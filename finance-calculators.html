Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
Are the tax calculators updated for FY 2025-26?
Yes, our Income Tax and Salary calculators are updated with the latest tax slabs (New and Old Regime) for the financial year 2025-2026.
Is my financial data safe?
Absolutely. All calculations happen inside your browser using JavaScript. We do not store or transmit your salary or loan details to any server.
How is the EMI calculated?
We use the standard reducing balance method formula: E = P x r x (1+r)^n / ((1+r)^n - 1), which is used by all major Indian banks (SBI, HDFC, ICICI).
