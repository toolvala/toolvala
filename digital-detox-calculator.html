What Is a Digital Detox?

A digital detox is a conscious break from digital devices such as smartphones, laptops, tablets, and social media platforms. The goal is to reduce digital overload, reclaim time, improve mental clarity, and restore a healthy relationship with technology.

Using a digital detox calculator allows you to quantify your habits. Instead of guessing whether you are addicted to screens, this calculator shows measurable insights into how much time is consumed digitally and what it costs you annually.

Why Screen Time Awareness Is Critical

Research shows that excessive screen time is linked to anxiety, sleep disorders, reduced attention span, eye strain, and lower productivity. Many people underestimate how frequently they check their phones — often hundreds of times per day.

This screen time addiction calculator reveals hidden patterns and helps you take back control before burnout occurs.
