The Digital Detox Calculator is a free online tool to measure your daily screen time, phone addiction level, and social media usage. It helps you understand how digital habits affect your productivity, sleep quality, and mental health.

What Is Digital Detox?

A digital detox means reducing unnecessary screen time to regain focus, reduce anxiety, and improve digital wellbeing. Using a screen time calculator gives clarity instead of guesswork.

Why Screen Time Awareness Matters

Excessive screen usage increases stress, eye strain, sleep disorders, and reduces attention span. A phone addiction test like this helps identify unhealthy usage patterns early.

Benefits of Reducing Screen Time

  • Better sleep quality
  • Improved focus and productivity
  • Reduced anxiety and mental fatigue
  • More time for real-life activities
