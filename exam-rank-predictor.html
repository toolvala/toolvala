How Does Exam Rank Predictor Work?

This rank predictor calculator first converts your marks into percentage. Based on the selected difficulty level, it estimates your percentile. Finally, the percentile is converted into an approximate rank using the total number of candidates.

Why Use an Exam Rank Predictor?

An exam rank predictor helps students analyze performance, set realistic expectations, plan counseling choices, and decide future preparation strategies. It is especially useful immediately after exams when official results are not yet declared.

Important Disclaimer

This tool provides an estimated rank only. Actual rank may differ due to normalization, reservation policies, tie-breaking rules, and official exam authority calculations.
