What is Image Compressor by KB?

The Image Compressor by KB is a free online tool designed to reduce the size of your images to exact KB targets like 20KB, 50KB, 100KB, or even custom sizes. This ensures that your images meet upload restrictions on websites, government portals, online forms, and social media platforms.

Why Compress Images?

Large image files can slow down websites, fail to upload on platforms with size restrictions, or consume excessive storage. Compressing images without losing quality is essential for students, professionals, businesses, and anyone who shares digital images online.

Key Features of This Tool

  • Exact KB Compression: Compress images to predefined KB targets including 20KB, 50KB, and 100KB for specific upload requirements.
  • Bulk Upload: Select multiple images at once and compress them simultaneously to save time.
  • Auto Resize: Automatically adjusts image dimensions to ensure smaller file size without losing clarity.
  • Custom KB Size: Set any KB target as per your requirement.
  • Offline & Private: All processing happens in your browser. No image leaves your device.
  • Preview & Download: See compressed image previews and download them individually.

How to Compress Images in KB

  1. Select one or more images using the Select Images button.
  2. Choose a predefined target size (20KB, 50KB, 100KB) or set a custom size in KB.
  3. Enable or disable auto resize as needed. Auto resize is recommended for larger images.
  4. Click the Compress Images button to start compression.
  5. Preview the compressed images and their sizes. Download individual images using the provided button.
