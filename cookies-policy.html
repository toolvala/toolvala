Cookies Policy

This policy explains how Toolvala uses cookies and similar technologies.

Last Updated: December 1, 2024

Important Notice: Toolvala is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising purposes. Any cookies that may be present are either essential for the website to function or come from third-party services we use (like fonts and icons).

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

1.1 Types of Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Collect information about how visitors use a website
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements

Toolvala only uses essential cookies (if any) and does not use functional, analytics, or advertising cookies.

2. How Toolvala Uses Cookies

Unlike most websites, Toolvala has a minimal approach to cookies:

2.1 No Tracking Cookies

We do not use cookies to:

  • Track your browsing behavior
  • Collect analytics data
  • Show personalized advertisements
  • Remember your preferences (since no login is required)

2.2 Essential Cookies (If Any)

If cookies are present on Toolvala, they would be:

  • Hosting Provider Cookies: GitHub Pages may use cookies for load balancing or security
  • Third-Party Service Cookies: From Google Fonts or Font Awesome for font/icon delivery

We do not set any cookies ourselves for Toolvala's functionality.

4. Third-Party Cookies

4.1 Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts to display typography on Toolvala. When you visit our site, Google may set cookies related to font delivery. For more information, please see Google's Privacy Policy.

4.2 Font Awesome

We use Font Awesome for icons, loaded from their Content Delivery Network (CDN). Font Awesome may use cookies for performance optimization. For more information, please see Font Awesome's Privacy Policy.

4.3 GitHub Pages

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may use cookies for operational purposes such as load balancing and security. For more information, please see GitHub's Privacy Statement.

5. Managing Cookies

You have several options for managing cookies on Toolvala:

5.1 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Delete all cookies stored on your device
  • Block all or certain types of cookies
  • Set preferences for different websites

Here's how to manage cookies in popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

5.2 Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies on Toolvala:

  • Website will still function: All tools will work normally
  • Fonts may not display correctly: Text may appear in default system fonts
  • Icons may not display: Some icons may not load properly
  • No impact on tool functionality: All tools will work as intended

Since we don't use cookies for core functionality, disabling cookies has minimal impact on your Toolvala experience.

6. Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, there are other similar technologies that websites may use:

6.1 Local Storage

Some Toolvala tools may use your browser's local storage to:

  • Remember tool settings temporarily (during your session)
  • Improve performance by caching certain data

Local storage data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us. You can clear local storage through your browser settings.

6.2 Session Storage

Similar to local storage but cleared when you close your browser tab.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain rights regarding cookies:

  • Right to be informed: This policy fulfills this right
  • Right to access: You can see what cookies are set
  • Right to withdraw consent: You can manage cookies through browser settings

7.2 Under CCPA/CPRA

If you are a California resident, you have the right to opt-out of the sale of your personal information. Since we don't use tracking cookies or sell data, this doesn't apply to Toolvala.

7.3 Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" feature that lets you tell websites you don't want to be tracked. Since Toolvala doesn't track users, we don't respond to Do Not Track signals differently.
