Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
How accurate is the Diaper Usage Calculator?
It uses standard medical averages (e.g., Newborns: 8-10/day, Toddlers: 4-5/day) to give you a highly accurate estimate for planning.
What is the Finish Date Calculator?
This unique tool takes your current diaper stock (e.g., 50 pieces) and your baby's age to tell you the exact date (e.g., "15th March") when you will run out.
Are these tools free?
Yes, all tools are 100% free for parents.
