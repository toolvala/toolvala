About Attendance Percentage Calculator

The Attendance Percentage Calculator is an essential online tool for students studying in schools, colleges, universities and competitive exam institutes. Attendance plays a critical role in exam eligibility in India. Most educational institutions require a minimum of 75% attendance to allow students to appear for examinations. This free tool helps students calculate their exact attendance percentage within seconds.

Why Attendance Percentage Matters

Attendance percentage reflects a student’s consistency and discipline. Universities, boards, and competitive exams use attendance as a key criterion for internal assessment and exam eligibility. Low attendance can lead to penalties, fines, or even exam disqualification.

How Attendance Percentage Is Calculated

The formula used in this tool is simple and accurate:

(Classes Attended ÷ Total Classes Conducted) × 100

For example, if total classes are 120 and attended classes are 90, then attendance percentage is 75%.

75% Attendance Rule in India

Most Indian boards such as CBSE, state boards, and universities follow the 75% attendance rule. Some institutions allow relaxation with medical certificates or valid reasons, but students should always aim to maintain higher attendance.
