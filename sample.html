Privacy and Browser-Based Security

One of the core strengths of Toolvala.in is our commitment to user privacy. We understand that your PDFs and personal photos contain sensitive information. That is why the majority of our tools, including the PDF Compressor and Image Converters, are designed to work **client-side**. This means all the processing happens inside your own web browser. Your files are never uploaded to our servers, and we never see your data. This makes Toolvala one of the safest platforms on the internet today.
