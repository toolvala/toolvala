What Is a UPI Payment Link?

A UPI payment link is a specially formatted URL that starts with upi://pay. When clicked, it automatically opens installed UPI apps such as Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, or Amazon Pay.

Example UPI Payment Link:

upi://pay?pa=example@upi&pn=Toolvala&am=500&tn=Payment
