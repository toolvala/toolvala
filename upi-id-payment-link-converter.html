Expert Guide: UPI Payment Links and QR Technology

Unified Payments Interface (UPI) is the backbone of India's digital economy. Our generator allows you to create secure deep links using the NPCI standard upi://pay URI scheme. This is 100% free and works with Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and BHIM.

Why Use a Generated QR Code?

For shopkeepers and freelancers, accuracy is vital. By generating a dynamic QR code with a pre-set amount, you eliminate "typo" risks where customers enter the wrong amount. This speeds up the checkout process and ensures instant settlement into your bank account.

Security & Privacy

This tool runs entirely on your device. We do not transmit your UPI ID or payment amounts to any server. Since UPI requires a 4 or 6-digit PIN on the sender's phone, these links are a safe way to request money without exposing sensitive bank details.
